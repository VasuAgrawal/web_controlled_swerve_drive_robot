1 /* A physical module consists of a wheel mounted on a drive motor,
2  * which is mounted in a clevice made of acrylic. The clevice has a
3  * hex bore and has a gear attached to the top, which also have hex
4  * bores. There is a hex shaft coming out of a steering motor; the
5  * hex shaft penetrates both the gear and the clevice, thus linking
6  * the motons. Attached to the gear is another gear, whose axle is
7  * the shaft of a potentiomter. Thus, rotation of the steer motor
8  * will turn the potentiometer for feedback, and will turn the
9  * clevice for the drive motor.
10  *
11  * Each module is linked in code to its corresponding motor shield.
12  * This means that this code module is completely capable of
13  * controlling the steering and driving of a module precisely.
14  */
